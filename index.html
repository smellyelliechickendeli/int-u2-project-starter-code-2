Why Music Is Necessary Daily



Music is the essence of life. The rhythm keeps us going and motivated everyday, and reflects our moods. Although you may not be one to listen to music often, or at all, we want to fix that.
Here is how listening to music benefits you, and how to get around the financial or advertisment obstacle.

Improves Anxiety and Depression

Listening to music, especially classical, reduces your heart rate (depending on the tempo), cortisol levels, and even blood pressure. These three are main factors and symptoms to anxiety. Likewise, listening to music improves your mood and relieves sadness; depression or not.

Betters The Physical State

  • Heart Health: Music changes the heart rate depending on the tempo of the song.
  • Fatigue: Choosing to listen to your personal preferences or calming songs decreases your mental fatigue.
